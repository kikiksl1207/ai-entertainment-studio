## Notion Summary

Ha Yoona is a 26-year-old Korean female trend icon, SNS and short-form queen, and trendy actor-line character. Her core mood is urban, cool, straightforward, witty, hip, and slightly unpredictable. She should feel like a high-fashion street beauty rather than a soft idol or innocent romantic character.

## Face Standard

- Sharp slim V-line jaw with a narrow, defined chin.
- Chic, slightly upturned cat-like eyes.
- Confident direct gaze with a sly or crooked smirk.
- Dark brown textured hush cut with light wispy bangs.
- Vivid red lips, crisp cat-eye liner, and matte flawless skin.
- Keep the face close to the selected thumb standard: sharp cat-eye, red lip, dark hush cut, and edgy confidence.

## Body And Proportions

- 26-year-old Korean woman.
- 171cm runway/supermodel impression.
- Slim, toned, long-legged proportions.
- Street-fashion full-body cuts should preserve long straight legs and strong posture.

## Outfit, Color, And Props

- Main styling: black leather cropped jacket, fitted crop top, wide cargo pants, chunky boots.
- Accent styling: red leather/bomber, electric blue top, neon green athletic top, chrome/silver chains.
- Props and details: tinted sunglasses, headphones, motorcycle, chrome car/street set, neon city lights.
- Palette: edgy black, vivid red, chrome silver, electric blue, neon green accent.

## Cover Choice

`cover.png` was selected because it clearly shows Ha Yoona as a public-facing representative image: full-body street high-fashion styling, black leather and cargo silhouette, neon urban setting, long-legged model proportions, and a confident cat-eye expression.

## Thumb Choice

`thumb.png` was selected because it is the strongest face anchor for app cards and profile use. The eyes, red lips, sunglasses, dark hush cut, and sharp jawline remain readable at small sizes.

## Reference Selection

The final references prioritize face consistency over sheer variety. The pack includes:

- Full-body street high-fashion cuts.
- Bust and close-up face anchors.
- Motorcycle, car, skate/street, studio, and neon city variations.
- A few action and pose variations that still preserve Ha Yoona's sharp face standard.

The final set intentionally uses 24 references, exceeding the original minimum because the additional images passed the stricter face consistency review.

## Hold And Reject Rationale

Excluded images were removed for one or more of the following:

- Male-character drift.
- Face drift into a different woman.
- Softer idol-like, rounder, or more innocent facial impression.
- Overly bright/cute expression that weakened the sharp Ha Yoona identity.
- Off-brief noir/suit styling or weak app-gallery role separation.
- Any image that risked confusing Ha Yoona with existing public characters.

## Separation From Existing Characters

Do not soften Ha Yoona toward Yoon Serin, Han Seoyul, Choi Seojin, Park Doa, Seo Yuan, or other existing public characters. Her distinguishing traits are:

- Sharper cat-like eyes.
- More urban and street-fashion styling.
- Bolder leather/cargo/chrome silhouette.
- Vivid red lip and stronger cat-eye makeup.
- Witty, direct, confident energy instead of innocent, elegant, soft, or traditionally lovely moods.

## Future Seed/API Notes

- Face consistency should be weighted above outfit variety.
- Use `thumb.png` and `reference-final-16.png` / `reference-final-24.png` style face anchors when judging future generations.
- Keep prompt terms such as `sharp slim V-line jaw`, `upturned cat-like eyes`, `dark-brown textured hush cut`, `vivid red lips`, and `edgy urban street high-fashion`.
- Avoid prompts that emphasize innocent beauty, puppy-like eyes, pastel styling, bridal elegance, soft idol cuteness, or generic daily outfits.
- Reject any output that resembles a real public figure, shows excessive exposure, includes logos/text/watermarks, or drifts into another character identity.
