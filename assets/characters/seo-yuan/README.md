# Seo Yuan Character Asset Pack

## Character Baseline

- Character: Seo Yuan / 서유안
- Source: Notion character sheet v1
- Mood: natural luxury, graceful Korean premium model, skincare and fragrance commercial muse
- Face anchor: soft long oval Korean face, warm brown almond eyes, small softly rounded nose tip, gentle balanced lips with a calm slight smile, long dark brown hair, flawless transparent dewy skin
- Palette: pure white, soft ivory, oatmeal beige, pale sky blue, warm grey
- Styling: minimal silk blouse or dress, ivory knit, oatmeal cardigan, beige trench, restrained beauty/lifestyle styling

## Included Assets

- `cover.png`: primary skincare-style cover portrait
- `thumb.png`: cover-derived crop for identity consistency
- `reference-final-01.png` - `reference-final-20.png`: final reference set

Total: 22 PNG assets.

## Selection Notes

The final set was selected by prioritizing face consistency over pose variety. The first pass included several attractive but weaker identity matches; these were removed after a face-crop audit.

Kept groups:

- Beauty / close portrait references
- Home living and fragrance lifestyle references
- Minimal full-body references for silhouette and outfit shape

Removed from the prior selected set:

- `ref-05`: rounder and softer face impression than the cover
- `ref-09`: strongest mismatch in eye, nose, and face balance
- `ref-10`: sharper V-line and different eye-nose balance
- `ref-11`: more mature and slender side-face impression
- `ref-19`: trench portrait looked slightly more mature than the anchor face

## Usage Notes

- Use `cover.png` and `thumb.png` as the public identity anchors.
- Treat the full-body images as silhouette and wardrobe references; face matching is strongest in the close and bust portraits.
- Avoid mixing this character with black glam styling, smoky makeup, idol stage styling, heavy jewelry, or sharper Westernized facial features.
- Future generations should preserve the same face anchor and keep the styling in the clean white, ivory, oatmeal, and pale-blue natural-luxury range.
